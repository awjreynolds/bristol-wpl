# Parking Base Methodology

Status: Stage 4A method control.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Owner: Spatial/Data Agent.

This is not a parking-base estimate and contains no live inventory records.

## Method Sequence

1. Confirm permitted source datasets, licences and data classifications.
2. Build or acquire candidate site and premises base.
3. Link UPRN, LLPG, VOA, planning or manual references.
4. Assign candidate premises to boundary option IDs using documented spatial joins.
5. Identify occupiers, associated organisations and third-party parking providers where lawful and evidenced.
6. Create parking area records with source and confidence.
7. Record observations or declarations with count method and uncertainty.
8. Classify legal parking-place category and possible exemptions.
9. Reconcile inventory records to licence, inspection, enforcement and appeal controls.
10. Produce officer XLSX views from canonical data or reconcile officer edits back into canonical data.

## Permitted Count Methods

- site survey;
- employer return;
- planning record;
- aerial imagery where licensed and appropriate;
- business-rates linkage;
- modelled estimate with explicit uncertainty;
- mixed method.

Nottingham categories, assumptions or elasticities must not be transferred automatically to Bristol.

## Uncertainty

Every count must record:

- total places;
- workplace parking places;
- non-exempt estimate;
- exempt estimate;
- low and high uncertainty bounds;
- count method;
- count confidence;
- source IDs and hashes.

## Current Limitation

No source data, survey data, declarations, inspection records or parking counts have been loaded. Stage 4 remains blocked.
