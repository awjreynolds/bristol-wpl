## Required Resolution Controls

Any simulated committee report seeking OBC, consultation, FBC, final order or submission approval must include a decision box that states:

1. the statutory power and product being considered;
2. whether the decision is a key decision and where it appears on the Forward Plan;
3. the exact decision sought from Transport and Connectivity Committee;
4. whether Full Council is required or not required, with source-bounded reasons;
5. the officer(s) authorised to prepare, publish, sign, seal, submit or implement documents;
6. Monitoring Officer, Chief Finance Officer and equality/public-law advice status;
7. conditions precedent before any order comes into force;
8. no-overclaim wording that distinguishes development, consultation, order-making, submission and Secretary of State confirmation.

## Stage 2D Finding

Stage 2D controls the business-case decision route for simulation drafting: Transport and Connectivity Committee is the best-evidenced internal member route for OBC approval, progression beyond OBC/consultation launch and FBC approval. It does not close the final WPL licensing-scheme order-maker or statutory submitter route. `RISK-0001` and `ISS-0001` remain P0, but the unresolved point is now limited to the final order and submission route rather than earlier OBC/FBC member-route control.

Stage 2 remains no-go because WECA/MCA current-law role is still P0 and later spatial, appraisal, consultation, operations and finance gates remain open.

## Prohibited Language
