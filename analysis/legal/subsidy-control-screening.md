# Subsidy Control Screening

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice or professional sign-off.

## Question

Could exemptions, discounts, mitigation payments or transport interventions create subsidy-control issues?

## Current Law And Evidence

The repo has no scheme-specific subsidy source pack. The requirement to screen exemptions, discounts, mitigation payments, employer support measures and funded transport interventions for selective economic advantage comes from `CODEX_MASTER_PROMPT.md` section 11.

## Analysis

Stage 2 screening inference: potential subsidy-control touchpoints could include:

- employer exemptions or discounts targeted at specific sectors or places;
- mitigation grants or support for employers;
- transport services funded by net proceeds that benefit selected operators;
- procurement of services below market terms;
- transitional relief or hardship schemes.

## Uncertainty

No exemption, discount, mitigation or investment package exists.

## Scheme Consequence

No package can be described as subsidy-safe.

## Evidence Required

- Subsidy-control source pack.
- Screening log for each measure.
- Transparency/referral assessment.
- Decision owner and legal reviewer.

## Decision

Make subsidy screening mandatory before OBC preferred option and FBC.

## Professional Reviewer

Simulated Subsidy Control Review Agent.

## Risk If Unresolved

The package may create unlawful or unreported selective economic advantage.
