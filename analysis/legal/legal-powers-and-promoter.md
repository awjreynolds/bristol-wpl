# Legal Powers And Promoter

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice or professional sign-off.

## Question

Who has power to promote, make and submit a Bristol Workplace Parking Levy scheme order?

## Current Law

Transport Act 2000 workplace parking levy powers sit in the workplace parking licensing scheme framework. The repo evidence supports treating an initial scheme as a licensing scheme order requiring confirmation before coming into force. `SRC-LEG-0003` supports the confirmation control. `SRC-LEG-0011` supports only a narrow RPI-only variation exemption.

## Analysis

Bristol City Council has an evidenced public decision trail for Stage One Development and OBC work, not an evidenced final promoter/order-maker route. `SRC-BCC-0004` and `SRC-BCC-0022` approve Stage One Development and OBC option testing and authorise Stage 1 contract activity, but they do not prove the constitutional route for making a WPL order or submitting it for confirmation.

The WECA/MCA current-law position is not settled. `SRC-LEG-0009` and `SRC-LEG-0010` must be mapped against the current consolidated devolution position, WECA/MCA constitution and Bristol constitution before any no-role, consent-required, concurrent-function or transferred-function conclusion is made.

## Uncertainty

P0 remains open for Bristol competence, order-maker, delegation and submission route. P0 remains open for WECA/MCA role.

## Scheme Consequence

The simulation must not present a preferred scheme, statutory consultation launch, FBC readiness or statutory submission route as legally settled.

## Evidence Required

- Current Bristol City Council constitution and executive/committee/delegation scheme.
- Current WECA/MCA constitution and delegation scheme.
- Consolidated current devolution instruments affecting local transport and WPL-related functions.
- Written route note identifying maker, submitter, recipient of charges, enforcement operator and accountable finance body.

## Decision

Stage 2 technical baseline only. Keep promoter/order-maker as unresolved P0.

## Professional Reviewer

Simulated Legal Review Agent.

## Risk If Unresolved

Any OBC/FBC recommendation could name the wrong authority, wrong approval body or wrong statutory submission route.
