# Schedule 12 Net Proceeds Accounting Policy

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice or professional sign-off.

## Question

What accounting controls are needed for WPL net proceeds?

## Current Law

`SRC-LEG-0004` requires annual accounts and statements for relevant schemes. `SRC-LEG-0012` requires accruals-basis determination of amounts received and expenses incurred, proper practices, interest on balances and deduction of expenses and applied net proceeds from receipts.

## Analysis

The accounting policy must cover:

- accruals basis;
- proper practices;
- receipts and expenses;
- year attribution;
- depreciation and impairment;
- deferred charges;
- capital financing charges;
- interest on balances;
- refunds;
- bad debt;
- penalty income;
- audit trail;
- ring-fenced account controls;
- annual account publication route.

## Uncertainty

No financial model, ledger design or accountable-body decision exists.

## Scheme Consequence

The financial case cannot claim affordability, borrow-against-revenue readiness or statutory-account readiness.

## Evidence Required

- Finance model and ledger specification.
- Accountable-body decision.
- Annual statement format.
- Audit and reconciliation procedure.

## Decision

Create accounting policy as a Stage 7/Stage 11 dependency.

## Professional Reviewer

Simulated Finance Review Agent and Legal Review Agent.

## Risk If Unresolved

Revenue could be misstated, misapplied or unreconciled to statutory proceeds requirements.
