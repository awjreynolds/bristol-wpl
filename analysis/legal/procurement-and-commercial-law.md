# Procurement And Commercial Law

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice or professional sign-off.

## Question

What procurement and commercial-law controls are needed?

## Current Law And Evidence

`SRC-BCC-0004` and `SRC-BCC-0022` evidence Stage 1 procurement/contract authority linked to development work. They do not evidence procurement authority for implementation, licensing operations, enforcement services, IT systems or long-term delivery contracts.

## Analysis

The commercial case must distinguish:

- consultancy/development contracts;
- employer survey/data services;
- IT/licensing platform;
- payment processing;
- enforcement and recovery services;
- appeals administration;
- communications and consultation services.

## Uncertainty

No final make/buy strategy, procurement route, contract packaging, operating model or implementation authority exists.

## Scheme Consequence

Commercial readiness cannot be claimed.

## Evidence Required

- Procurement strategy.
- Contract register.
- Route-to-market analysis.
- Subsidy-control and data-processing screening.
- Implementation delegation decision.

## Decision

Stage 2 can record procurement dependencies only.

## Professional Reviewer

Simulated Commercial Review Agent and Procurement Law Review Agent.

## Risk If Unresolved

Delivery may breach procurement rules, understate lead times or rely on unapproved operating contracts.
