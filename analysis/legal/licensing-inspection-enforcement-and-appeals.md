# Licensing Inspection Enforcement And Appeals

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice or professional sign-off.

## Question

What operating controls are required for licensing, inspection, enforcement and appeals?

## Current Law

`SRC-LEG-0011` regulations 4 to 16 set out licence-charge liability, penalty-charge circumstances, PCN content, representations, cancellation, rejection, appeal to county court, charge certificates, recovery and service.

## Analysis

The statutory operating model must include:

- licence application and renewal;
- evidence of workplace parking places and liable person;
- inspection/right-of-entry procedure;
- PCN evidence standard;
- PCN template and service route;
- representations intake and evidence review;
- notice of acceptance/rejection;
- county court appeal handling;
- charge certificate and recovery procedure;
- cancellation and fresh PCN control;
- audit logging and data retention.

## Uncertainty

No operating procedure, PCN template, service policy, appeal/recovery workflow or DPIA exists.

## Scheme Consequence

Operational readiness is blocked. Consultation materials must not suggest the enforcement service is built.

## Evidence Required

- Licensing SOP.
- PCN and notice templates.
- Service policy.
- Appeals/recovery workflow.
- DPIA and records of processing activity.

## Decision

Keep as Stage 4/Stage 8/Stage 11 dependency.

## Professional Reviewer

Simulated Operations Design Agent, Legal Review Agent and Data Protection Review Agent.

## Risk If Unresolved

The authority could be unable to enforce fairly, lawfully or consistently.
