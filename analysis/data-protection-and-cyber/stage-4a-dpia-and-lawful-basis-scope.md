# Stage 4A DPIA and Lawful-Basis Scope

Status: simulation control scope.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Owner: Data Protection Review Agent.

This is not a DPIA, privacy notice, lawful-basis decision, controller assessment or Data Protection Officer sign-off.

## Trigger

The boundary and parking-inventory work may involve premises, occupier, employer, contact, vehicle, inspection, enforcement, representation and appeal data. Some records may be commercially sensitive or personal data.

## Required Before Data Collection

Before live employer, premises, vehicle, inspection, declaration or enforcement data is collected or loaded, the programme must create:

- DPIA scope and full DPIA;
- records of processing activity inputs;
- lawful-basis and statutory-gateway table;
- controller, joint-controller and processor assessment;
- data-flow map;
- privacy notices;
- retention and disposal rules;
- data-sharing and processor-contract controls;
- public-release redaction policy;
- human-review route for eligibility and enforcement decisions.

## Repository Control

Restricted, personal or commercially sensitive records must not be committed to normal repo paths. Normal repo files may contain only public, synthetic, aggregated or non-sensitive control data unless a later data-classification manifest authorises otherwise.

## Current No-Go

Stage 4A does not authorise employer survey launch, inspection, declaration processing, enforcement preparation or public release of inventory data.
