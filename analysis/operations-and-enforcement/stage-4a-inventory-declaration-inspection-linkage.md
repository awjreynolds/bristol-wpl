# Stage 4A Inventory, Declaration and Inspection Linkage

Status: simulation control scope.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Owner: Operations Design Agent.

This is not an operating procedure, PCN template, service policy, appeal workflow or operational readiness sign-off.

## Purpose

The parking inventory must be designed so future licence, declaration, inspection, enforcement and appeal records can be traced back to the evidence used to identify workplace parking places.

## Required Linkages

Future records must link:

- site and premises records to UPRN, LLPG, VOA, planning or manual references;
- premises and occupier records to valid-from and valid-to dates;
- parking areas to geometry references and boundary option IDs;
- observations to source IDs, source hashes, count method and confidence;
- declarations to licence year, declared places and evidence files;
- inspections to legal power or authority, evidence and outcome;
- exemptions and discounts to legal or policy basis;
- PCN, representation, appeal and recovery records to the relevant licence, inspection or declaration record;
- every material change to an audit event.
