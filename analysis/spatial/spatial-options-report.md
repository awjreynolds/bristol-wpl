- whole Bristol administrative area;
- defined sub-city zones;
- employment-cluster options;

## Required Appraisal Fields

Each option must record:

- legal certainty;
- alignment with objectives;
- chargeable-space base and uncertainty;
- traffic and mode-shift potential;
- public transport accessibility and capacity;
- cross-boundary travel;
- displacement and spillover;
- business and equality impacts;
- enforceability;
- data quality;
- implementation cost;
- revenue range;
- consultation and political risk;
- source and reviewer status.
