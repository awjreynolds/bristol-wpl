# Objectives and Critical Success Factors

Status: Stage 3A control placeholder.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Owner: Strategic Case Review Agent.

This file defines objective controls only. It does not approve objectives for OBC/FBC.

## Objective Rules

Future objectives must:

- be linked to evidenced problems;
- avoid prejudging WPL;
- be measurable where evidence permits;
- distinguish transport outcomes from revenue outcomes;
- recognise equality, business, environment and cross-boundary effects;
- be compatible with statutory net-proceeds transport-policy linkage.

Objectives must be outcome-based and intervention-neutral. Revenue may be handled as fiscal context or affordability constraint but must not become an objective that presupposes a WPL.

## Critical Success Factors

Future CSFs must test:

- strategic fit;
- legal feasibility;
- transport effectiveness;
- value for money;
- affordability;
- revenue resilience;
- deliverability;
- operational enforceability;
- equality and social acceptability;
- cross-boundary coherence;
- consultation defensibility;
- environmental performance;
- adaptability.
