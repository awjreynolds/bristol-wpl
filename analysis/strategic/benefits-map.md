# Benefits Map

Status: Stage 3A control placeholder.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Owner: Strategic Case Review Agent.

This map identifies candidate benefit areas only. It does not quantify or approve benefits.
It does not support BCR, VFM, mode-shift, congestion-reduction, distributional-effect or investment-benefit claims.

## Candidate Benefit Areas

- reduced workplace parking demand;
- mode shift where supported by evidence;
- improved bus journey time or reliability from funded interventions;
- active-travel accessibility;
- reduced congestion where modelled and evidenced;
- air quality and carbon impacts where modelled and evidenced;
- revenue for transport policies or proposals;
- distributional or place benefits where evidenced.

## Required Benefit Controls

Every future benefit must record:

- objective link;
- measure;
- baseline;
- target;
- attribution route classified as WPL-only, transport-investment-only or combined-package;
- dependency on WPL-only, investment-only or combined effects;
- owner;
- source evidence;
- uncertainty;
- reviewer;
- status.

Gross levy receipts must be treated as a transfer/public cash-flow item, not an economic benefit.
