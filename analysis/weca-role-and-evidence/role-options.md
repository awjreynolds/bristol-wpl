# WECA/MCA Role Options

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not legal advice, WECA/MCA approval or assurance sign-off.

## Purpose

This note defines controlled role options for WECA/MCA in the Bristol WPL simulation. It prevents the business case from assuming a role that is not evidenced.

## Controlled Options

| option_id | role option | what would evidence it | current repo classification | gate effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WECA-ROLE-01 | Strategic policy context only | Adopted regional strategies that mention demand management or WPL-type tools. | Supported by `SRC-WECA-0001`, `SRC-WECA-0003`, `SRC-WECA-0004`. | May be cited as context only. |
| WECA-ROLE-02 | Funding or assurance role | Formal decision or framework requirement where WECA/MCA funding streams are used for the WPL-funded package or development costs. | Possible, not settled. `SRC-WECA-0006` is relevant if funding engaged. | P1/P0 depending on funding dependency. |
| WECA-ROLE-03 | Delivery or coordination role | Formal agreement, memorandum, delivery plan or governance decision. | Not established. | Do not assume. |
| WECA-ROLE-04 | Statutory consent or concurrence role | Current law, constitution or formal decision requiring consent/concurrence. | P0 unresolved. `SRC-LEG-0009` and `SRC-LEG-0010` trigger review. | Blocks route conclusion. |
| WECA-ROLE-05 | Transferred or concurrent WPL function | Consolidated devolution law and constitution showing transferred or concurrent function. | P0 unresolved. | Blocks promoter conclusion. |
| WECA-ROLE-06 | Formal support | WECA/MCA committee decision, mayoral decision, formal consultation response or equivalent. | Not established. | Must not be claimed. |
| WECA-ROLE-07 | Formal objection | WECA/MCA committee decision, mayoral decision, formal consultation response or equivalent. | Not established. | Must not be claimed. |
| WECA-ROLE-08 | No direct role | Current law, constitutions and meeting-record search establishing no statutory, assurance, funding or delivery role. | Not established. | Must not be claimed. |

## Current Working Treatment

The simulation may cite WECA/MCA as strategic and assurance context only. It must not state that WECA/MCA has approved, rejected, consented to, sponsored or has no role in a Bristol WPL.

## Evidence Needed To Close P0

- Consolidated current West of England devolution function map.
- Current WECA/MCA constitution and delegations.
- Current Bristol constitution and delegation map.
- Meeting-record search for WPL/demand-management/Bristol WPL terms.
- Written classification of any funding or assurance dependency.
