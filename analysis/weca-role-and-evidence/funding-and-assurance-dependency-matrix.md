# WECA/MCA Funding And Assurance Dependency Matrix

Status: simulation drafting control. This is not funding approval, assurance approval, business-case approval or professional finance advice.

## Dependency Matrix

| Dependency | Trigger | Current evidence | Current control | Gate effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WPL development costs paid solely by Bristol | Bristol-only revenue, reserve or approved budget source with no MCA funding condition. | Bristol Stage One/OBC funding is evidenced as CAZ Fund: `SRC-BCC-0004` lines 204-211 and `SRC-BCC-0014` lines 253-260. | WECA/MCA funding approval not inferred. | Still check statutory role separately. |
| MCA funding for WPL development | Any Single Pot, CRSTS, local growth, BSIP or other MCA funding pays for WPL studies, modelling, consultation, procurement or implementation. | No repo-local formal MCA WPL funding decision found. | Funding dependency remains open until source of funds is classified. | Blocks finance and management cases if triggered. |
| MCA funding for WPL-funded transport package | Any package funded from WPL net proceeds also uses MCA funds or match funding. | Possible only; no selected package or funding stack exists. | Each package needs funding-source and assurance-route row. | Blocks OBC/FBC package readiness if triggered. |
| MCA assurance framework | MCA-funded or MCA-assured scheme, or transport scheme requiring OAR/ASR/MCA review. | `SRC-WECA-0006` lines 117-136, 373-391 and 1983-1986; `SRC-WECA-0007` lines 718-724 and 918-976 describe funding and business-case controls. | Use as conditional assurance control only. | P1 unless it becomes a named funding dependency; then P0 until route approved. |
| CRSTS / Single Pot / early works | Any request for Single Pot, CRSTS or early-works funding. | `SRC-WECA-0007` lines 116-120 and 1042-1046. | Funding route must be classified before any assurance or approval claim. | P0 if triggered and not approved. |
| Local Transport Plan / strategy alignment | Regional strategic context or local transport policy linkage. | `SRC-WECA-0001`, `SRC-WECA-0003`, `SRC-WECA-0004`, `SRC-WECA-0012`. | Strategic alignment may be cited. | Does not prove approval or consent. |
| CAZ repayment / interaction | WPL development or net proceeds interact with CAZ repayment or clean-air funding obligations. | Current Stage 2E sources do not settle this. | Add finance/legal question before OBC financial case. | Open. |
| Mass transit, bus or cross-boundary package | WPL net proceeds fund packages integrated with WECA/MCA programmes. | Strategic sources mention bus and demand-management context only. | Package-level interface matrix required. | Open until package selected and funding interfaces mapped. |
| Bristol programme-board governance | Bristol Transport Board or WPL Steering Group reviews or coordinates key programme decisions. | `SRC-BCC-0006` lines 1264-1272. | Treat as programme governance only, not statutory approval or MCA approval. | Open until a current approved governance route is evidenced. |

## Required OBC/FBC Controls

Every WPL-funded package or development-cost line must record:

1. funding source;
2. whether MCA funding is used;
3. whether MCA assurance applies;
4. whether OAR/ASR or MCA review is required;
5. whether any WECA/MCA approval, funding agreement or delegation is required;
6. whether Bristol Transport Board, WPL Steering Group or other programme-board review is advisory, delegated or decision-making;
7. whether the evidence is a formal decision, strategy context, officer engagement or unresolved assumption;
8. whether the funding route creates conditions before consultation, FBC, procurement, implementation or statutory submission.

## No-Overclaim Control

Do not describe MCA assurance relevance as WPL consent or approval. Do not describe a Bristol-funded WPL development stage as free from WECA/MCA role until statutory, constitutional and funding-dependency checks are complete.
