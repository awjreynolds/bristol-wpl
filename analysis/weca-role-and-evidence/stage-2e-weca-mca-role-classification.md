# Stage 2E WECA/MCA Role Classification

Status: simulated legal, governance and funding-assurance due-diligence artefact. This is not legal advice, WECA/MCA approval, Bristol officer advice, statutory confirmation, funding approval or professional assurance.

## Purpose

Stage 2E tests the working hypothesis that WECA/MCA is currently evidenced as strategic and conditional funding/assurance context only, while Bristol carries the active public WPL decision trail. The test is deliberately conservative: absence of a located WECA/MCA WPL decision is not proof that WECA/MCA has no role.

## Controlled Wording

Permitted:

- WECA/MCA and West of England strategies are relevant strategic context for demand management, public transport funding and regional transport alignment.
- WECA/MCA assurance may become relevant if Bristol WPL development or WPL-funded transport packages use MCA funding streams or require MCA business-case assurance.
- Current repo evidence describes the OBC-stage development budget as funded from Bristol Clean Air Zone Fund, not as a formal WECA/MCA WPL funding decision.
- No repo-local source currently evidences formal WECA/MCA approval, support, objection, consent, sponsorship or funding decision for a Bristol WPL.
- Current scoped sources do not support a transferred or concurrent WECA/MCA WPL function, but that is not a final no-role conclusion.
- No repo-local source currently evidences a WECA/MCA WPL-specific consultation requirement or formal consultation response.
- The WECA/MCA current-law role is unresolved and must not be inferred from strategy sources.

Prohibited:

- WECA/MCA has approved, supported, objected to, consented to, funded or sponsored the Bristol WPL.
- WECA/MCA consent is required.
- WECA/MCA consent is not required.
- WECA/MCA has a transferred or concurrent WPL function.
- WECA/MCA has no role.
- Strategic alignment is formal approval.
- MCA assurance framework relevance is WPL statutory consent.
- Bristol Transport Board, WPL Steering Group or programme-board governance is a substitute for Bristol committee approval, WECA/MCA approval or Secretary of State confirmation.

## P0 That Remains Open

`RISK-0002` and `ISS-0002` remain P0. Stage 2E narrows the evidence classification, but it does not settle the current-law role. The remaining P0 is:

1. current-law function map after the 2026 amendments;
2. WECA/MCA constitution and delegation route mapped against Transport Act 2000 WPL powers;
3. Stage 2G bounded WECA/MCA public ModernGov meeting-record search has been completed, but it narrows only the public-record search condition and does not establish no-role, consent or funding-dependency status;
4. funding/assurance dependency classification for every proposed transport package and development-cost source;
5. explicit no-role, consent, concurrence, transferred-function or funding-dependency conclusion only if supported by the completed mapping.

## Stage 2E Finding

Stage 2E controls the language and classification of WECA/MCA evidence for simulation drafting. WECA/MCA is strategic context and possible conditional funding/assurance context only on current repo evidence. Stage 2 remains no-go because the current-law role and any funding/assurance dependency are not closed.
