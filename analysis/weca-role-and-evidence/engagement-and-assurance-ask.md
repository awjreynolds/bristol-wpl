# WECA/MCA Engagement And Assurance Ask

Status: Stage 2 technical baseline. Simulation only; not a live engagement request.

## Purpose

This is the controlled question set to use before any business case or statutory dossier claims a WECA/MCA role.

## Questions For WECA/MCA-Oriented Evidence Gathering

| ask_id | question | classification sought | reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| WECA-ASK-001 | Does current devolution law give WECA/MCA any transferred or concurrent WPL function? | statutory role | Closes or confirms P0. |
| WECA-ASK-002 | Does WECA/MCA constitution or delegation scheme require approval, consent, concurrence or notification for a Bristol WPL? | governance role | Closes or confirms P0. |
| WECA-ASK-003 | Would any WPL-funded transport package use WECA/MCA funding streams, assurance framework entry or programme-change approval? | funding/assurance role | Determines assurance dependency. |
| WECA-ASK-004 | Would CAZ repayment, CRSTS interfaces or mass-transit/bus-package interfaces require WECA/MCA decision-making? | funding/programme interface | Prevents unapproved net-proceeds assumptions. |
| WECA-ASK-005 | Has WECA/MCA considered, supported, objected to, funded or approved Bristol WPL in any formal meeting, decision or consultation response? | formal decision/support/objection | Prevents inference from silence. |
| WECA-ASK-006 | What evidence would WECA/MCA expect before any assurance review of a WPL-funded package? | assurance expectation | Supports OBC/FBC planning only. |
| WECA-ASK-007 | What governance route applies if Bristol WPL net proceeds fund cross-boundary regional transport interventions? | delivery/accounting role | Links statutory proceeds to transport policy. |

## Evidence Standard

Acceptable evidence must identify the source, date, decision-maker or officer role, power or policy basis, and whether the item is a formal decision, assurance requirement, informal engagement or no evidence found.
