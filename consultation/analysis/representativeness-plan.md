## Required Future Controls

Before response analysis, the project must define:

- stakeholder population and sampling frame assumptions;
- response-channel and digital-exclusion bias checks;
- employer-size, sector, geography, worker-impact and accessibility segments;
- weighting or sensitivity approach, including when weighting is inappropriate;
- minimum cell-size and disclosure controls;
- limits on interpreting self-selected consultation responses;
- reporting language that separates volume, strength of argument and evidence quality.
