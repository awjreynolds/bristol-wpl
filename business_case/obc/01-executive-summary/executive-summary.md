## Purpose

This executive summary sets out the simulated OBC position for a possible Bristol Workplace Parking Levy (WPL). It is written in a WECA-style Five Case Model format, using the Stage 32A exemplar corpus, but it remains simulation-only.

## Summary Position

The current simulation finding is no-go for OBC reliance. Bristol public evidence shows that a WPL OBC programme was in progress in March 2026 and that Bristol expected modelling, options and final OBC work to define any boundary, charges, thresholds, exemptions and operating hours (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4276-4295 and 4340-4349). That evidence does not show that the OBC was completed, approved or ready for reliance.

The September 2024 Bristol decision trail supports Stage One/OBC development funded through the Clean Air Zone Fund (`SRC-BCC-0003`, `SRC-BCC-0004`). The March 2026 report pack states that the OBC-development budget was GBP1m from the Clean Air Zone Fund and that future capital requirements would be considered during the FBC stage (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4506-4520). This is development-funding evidence only.

WECA/MCA material is relevant as regional strategy, assurance and possible package-funding context. It is not evidence that WECA/MCA approved, funded, sponsored, consented to, objected to or declined a role in Bristol WPL. Stage 2H requires every future WPL development-cost line, implementation-cost line and transport package to be classified before OBC/FBC reliance.

## Strategic Summary

**Cited.** Bristol's public WPL evidence frames the scheme as a potential demand-management and transport-investment mechanism intended to reduce congestion, support walking, cycling and public transport, and generate transport revenue (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4285-4288). The same source states that all net revenue would need to be reinvested in local transport after operating costs and that any new WPL must be approved by the Secretary of State before implementation (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4351-4361).

**Gap.** No authoritative boundary, workplace parking inventory, selected charge, threshold, exemption package, operating-hours proposal or investment package is held in this repo. These are P0/P1 blockers for a real OBC recommendation.
