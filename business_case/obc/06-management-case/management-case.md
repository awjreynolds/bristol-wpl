## Delivery Approach

**Proposal.** The management case should set out how Bristol would move from OBC evidence to consultation, FBC, statutory order, confirmation and implementation if future gates are passed. WECA-style exemplars use management sections to cover promoter and delivery arrangements, governance, programme, risks, dependencies, assurance, benefits realisation and monitoring (`SRC-WECA-0009` lines 1787-1807).

**Current Bristol WPL sequence.** The March 2026 Bristol update describes three broad stages: OBC, public consultation and FBC with approval, then implementation and operation (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4290-4294). It also states that formal public consultation is proposed only if the project progresses beyond the OBC stage and that a proposal would return to committee for approval and progression (`SRC-BCC-0015` lines 4468-4481).

**Gap.** This repo does not hold the real OBC, consultation approval, final order route, DfT engagement response, FBC, statutory dossier or implementation plan.
