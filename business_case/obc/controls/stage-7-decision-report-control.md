# Stage 7 OBC Decision Report Control

Status: simulation control.  
Date: 2026-06-26.

This is not an officer decision report. It is a control note for a future OBC decision report.

## Current Position

Stage 7 OBC gate remains blocked. No proceed recommendation may be made from the current repo evidence. This is the required no proceed recommendation wording for Stage 7A controls.

## Required Future Decision Report Structure

A future decision report must include:

- the decision sought and legal power relied on;
- whether the decision is only to continue development, to approve an OBC, to launch consultation, to approve spend, or to progress a statutory route;
- current Bristol decision-maker, signatory and submission route;
- WECA/MCA role, funding or assurance dependency classification;
- DfT engagement and Secretary of State confirmation distinction;
- summary of each Five Case Model section;
- open issues, risks, dependencies and evidence gaps;
- equalities, data-protection, accessibility and public-law consultation checks;
- financial affordability and net-proceeds treatment;
- options appraisal, BCR, VFM and uncertainty;
- Nottingham/comparator transferability caveats;
- red-team findings and disposition;
- explicit recommendation options including stop, pause, rework and proceed only if lawful and evidenced.

## Banned Decision Wording

The report must not say:

- the OBC is approved;
- the scheme is ready for consultation;
- the preferred scheme is selected;
- statutory submission is ready;
- WECA/MCA has a settled position;
- DfT has accepted the process;
- Nottingham proves Bristol impacts;
- open P0 blockers can be accepted as conditions.

Allowed wording is that Stage 7A creates decision-report controls only and that Stage 7 OBC gate remains blocked.
