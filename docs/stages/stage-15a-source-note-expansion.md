# Stage 15A Source Note Expansion

Status: complete as bounded source-note expansion.  
Date: 2026-06-26.

## What This Stage Does

Stage 15A extends the source-note system from the Stage 14A pilot into a larger legal and Bristol governance cohort. It makes the source base easier to review before future drafting and gives agents stricter boundaries for legal, governance, equality and statutory source use.

## What This Stage Does Not Do

It does not close `ISS-0007`, `EG-0024`, `EG-0038` or `EG-0043`. It does not verify every claim, provide legal advice, settle the Bristol order-maker route, settle WECA/MCA or DfT positions, assemble an OBC or FBC, launch consultation or authorise statutory submission.
