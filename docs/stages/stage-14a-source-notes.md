# Stage 14A Source Note Controls

Status: complete as source-note control pilot.
Date: 2026-06-26.

## What This Stage Does

Stage 14A creates the first editable source-note cohort and the controls for extending source notes across the evidence base. It makes it easier for officers, cabinet members, reviewers and agents to see what a source can support and what it must not be used to claim.

## What This Stage Does Not Do

It does not close `ISS-0007`. It does not verify every claim, complete the evidence base, replace legal advice, prove source currentness forever, assemble an OBC or FBC, launch consultation or authorise statutory submission.
