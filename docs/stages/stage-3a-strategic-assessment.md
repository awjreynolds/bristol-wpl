# Stage 3A Strategic Assessment Controls

Status: completed as control architecture. Stage 3 no-go remains.

## Purpose

Stage 3A creates the strategic assessment and investment-programme control architecture needed before any Strategic Case, preferred-way-forward recommendation, consultation proposition, investment package or OBC/FBC strategic case drafting.

## Key Data Points

- No preferred WPL scheme is selected.
- No investment package is selected.
- No package or cost line is approved, costed or funded.
- No benefit is quantified or approved.
- No final SMART objective set is approved.
- No baseline trend is sufficient to set targets or claim benefits.
- No WECA/MCA approval, support, objection, consent, funding, assurance approval or no-role conclusion is made.

## Key Findings

- Problem statements must distinguish observed Bristol evidence, comparator evidence, policy aspiration, forecast/modelled evidence, stakeholder claim, assumption, inference and gap.
- The strategic assessment must test WPL, non-WPL, investment-only and combined-package routes without assuming WPL is necessary.
- Objectives and CSFs must be intervention-neutral and must not use revenue as a predetermined strategic objective.
- Theory of change must separate WPL-only, transport-investment-only and combined-package effects.
- Benefits must not support BCR, VFM, mode-shift, congestion-reduction or distributional claims until evidence, baseline, target, uncertainty and reviewer status are recorded.
- Gross levy receipts must be treated as a transfer or public cash-flow item, not an economic benefit.
- Every package or cost line must record funding source, WECA/MCA trigger, assurance route, formal decision source and gate effect before reliance.

## What Stage 3A Does Not Claim

- It does not create a Strategic Case.
- It does not establish a preferred option.
- It does not select a WPL-funded package.
- It does not approve or fund any package or cost line.
- It does not close WECA/MCA role, consent, support, objection, funding or assurance issues.
- It does not support consultation launch, OBC/FBC recommendation or statutory submission.
