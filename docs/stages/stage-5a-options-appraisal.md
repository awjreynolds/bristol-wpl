# Stage 5A Options and Appraisal Controls

Status: completed as control architecture. Stage 5 no-go remains.

## Purpose

Stage 5A creates the options and appraisal control architecture needed before decision-grade OAR, ASR, ASST, model outputs, BCR, VFM category or OBC/FBC economic case drafting.

## Key Data Points

- No option is shortlisted.
- No preferred option exists.
- No ASR is agreed.
- No ASST impact is assessed.
- No model output exists.
- No BCR, NPV or value-for-money category exists.
- No revenue, boundary, parking-base, displacement or transport impact output exists.

## Key Findings

- Options must be generated from dimensions and include credible non-WPL, WPL-only, investment-only and combined-package alternatives.
- Gross levy receipts must not be treated as economic benefits.
- WPL-only, investment-only and combined-package effects must be separated.
- Nottingham assumptions cannot transfer automatically to Bristol.
- Current guidance reliance is not complete until individual TAG units, TAG databook, VFM framework, uncertainty guidance and source notes are acquired for the selected appraisal scope.
- ASR/ASST controls must exist before modelling or OBC economic drafting.

## What Stage 5A Does Not Claim

- It does not select a shortlist.
- It does not identify a preferred option.
- It does not provide BCR, NPV or VFM.
- It does not create model outputs.
- It does not evidence TAG compliance.
- It does not support consultation launch, OBC/FBC recommendation or statutory submission.
