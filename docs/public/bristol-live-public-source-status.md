## What Stage 28A Does Not Prove

Stage 28A does not prove:

- that all relevant public sources have been found;
- that sources are current today;
- that accessible URLs have unchanged or complete content;
- that media reporting is accurate;
- that press releases equal council decisions;
- that a public webpage equals statutory authority, consultation readiness, approval, funding commitment or WPL readiness;
- that paywalled or script-hydrated content was reviewed;
- that source relevance means claim support.
