# What This Repo Can And Cannot Tell You

Status: public/cabinet explainer.  
Date: 2026-06-26.

## In Plain Terms

This repo can tell you what has been checked in a simulation and what is still missing.

It cannot tell you that Bristol has approved, launched or submitted a Workplace Parking Levy.

| Known | Simulation Assumption | Gap | Do Not Claim |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bristol public records show early WPL development and OBC work. | Bristol City Council is treated as the proposed local licensing authority under investigation. | Final order-maker, statutory submitter and signatory route remain unresolved. | Bristol has approved implementation. |
| WECA/MCA sources can provide strategic and funding context. | WECA/MCA role remains under investigation. | Consent/no-role/funding-dependency position is not settled. | WECA/MCA has approved, objected, consented or has no role. |
| Nottingham is the main implemented UK WPL precedent. | Nottingham can inform questions and risks. | Bristol-specific transferability evidence is absent. | Nottingham outcomes will happen in Bristol. |
| Stage controls and validators exist. | Agent reviews simulate specialist challenge. | Human professional and public-authority approvals do not exist. | Agent sign-offs replace officers, lawyers, finance officers, modellers or elected members. |
| Source notes and claim summaries exist for current controlled material. | They help future drafters avoid unsupported claims. | Future drafting-specific claim summaries remain open. | Source notes or claim summaries prove every claim true. |
| Editable authoring guardrails exist. | Future outputs should remain editable and evidence-linked. | OBC, FBC, consultation, statutory and officer-review outputs are not assembled. | Editable templates are ready decision papers or authored officer-distribution PDFs. |
| Nottingham displacement and CPZ/RPZ risks are recorded. | Parking controls could be considered as possible future mitigation. | Boundary, parking inventory, residential baseline, option maps, costs, enforcement capacity and consultation evidence are absent. | CPZ/RPZ mitigation is selected, costed, consulted on or ready. |
| Stage 28A records Bristol live public-source coverage for `SRC-BCC-0001`, `SRC-BCC-0002` and media context `SRC-BCC-0020`. | The three user-provided Bristol links are source-typed and claim-limited. | Source truth, currentness, media accuracy, formal decision status, legal correctness and professional assurance remain open. | The selected public sources prove WPL readiness. |

## Reader Safeguard

If a statement sounds like approval, readiness, legal advice, professional sign-off or a selected scheme, check whether the linked evidence actually exists. If it does not, the correct reading is: this is a blocker, not a conclusion.
