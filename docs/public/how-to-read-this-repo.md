# How To Read This Repository

Status: public and cabinet orientation guide.  
Date: 2026-06-26.

## The Short Version

This repository is a simulation of the work needed before a Bristol Workplace Parking Levy could be considered safely.

It is not a Bristol City Council decision. It is not legal advice. It is not a consultation launch. It is not an Outline Business Case, Full Business Case or statutory submission.

## Five-Minute Route

1. Read `README.md` for the current no-go position.
2. Read `docs/officer/assurance-dashboard.md` for the red, amber and green controls.
3. Read `docs/officer/risk-gate-atlas.md` to see where the biggest risks sit in the workflow.
4. Use `docs/officer/document-map.md` only when you need a deeper file path.
5. Treat every agent sign-off as simulation-only unless a future real-world process replaces it with human professional and public-authority approval.

## What Green Means

Green means a control exists. It does not mean the WPL is approved or ready.

For example:

- green source-note coverage means source-note coverage exists;
- green claim-summary coverage means current claim summaries exist;
- green public-release controls mean publication wording is controlled;
- green authoring controls mean document scaffolds are guarded.

None of those green controls approve a charge, boundary, consultation, OBC, FBC or statutory submission.

## What Red Means

Red means a blocker prevents reliance.

The most important red blockers are:

- final Bristol legal/order-making route;
- WECA/MCA role;
- boundary and workplace parking inventory;
- appraisal and model outputs;
- consultation launch controls;
- FBC/statutory evidence;
- Bristol-specific Nottingham transferability evidence.

## Plain-English Safeguards

Do not read this repository as:

- council approval;
- legal advice;
- real officer approval;
- real consultant approval;
- an approved programme;
- approval to spend or procure;
- consultation launch authority;
- a selected boundary or charge;
- a completed parking inventory;
- ready CPZ/RPZ mitigation;
- proof that Nottingham outcomes transfer to Bristol.

The value of the repository is that it shows the questions, risks and checks that would need to be resolved before any of those claims could be made.

## Simulation Sign-Off

Simulation sign-off means an agent recorded a check inside this repo. It is not human professional advice, officer approval, legal approval, financial approval, consultation approval or elected-member authority.
