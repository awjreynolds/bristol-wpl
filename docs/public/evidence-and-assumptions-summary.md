# Evidence And Assumptions Summary

Status: public-safe simulation summary.  
Date: 2026-06-26.

## Known From Current Repo Evidence

- Bristol public records support Stage One development and OBC work, not implementation approval.
- An initial England-only WPL licensing scheme is controlled as requiring Secretary of State confirmation before coming into force, subject to unresolved Bristol submitter and signatory route questions.
- WECA/MCA material may currently be cited only as strategic context and possible conditional funding or assurance interface evidence.
- Nottingham is the main implemented UK WPL precedent in the acquired evidence base, but direct Bristol impact claims are blocked.
- Stage 18A records Nottingham/comparator transferability questions; those questions are not Bristol forecasts or ready parking-mitigation plans.

## Working Assumptions Only

- Bristol City Council is treated as the proposed local licensing authority under investigation for simulation drafting.
- Transport and Connectivity Committee is the controlled route for OBC progression and FBC member decisions, but not final WPL order-making.
- Nottingham lessons may inform questions and risk controls, not direct predictions.

## Known Gaps

- Final Bristol WPL order-maker, statutory submitter and signatory route.
- WECA/MCA role, consent, consultation-response and final funding-dependency position.
- DfT WPL-specific procedural expectations or response.
- WPL boundary, workplace parking inventory and displacement assessment.
- Current Nottingham charge, threshold and operating status are not refreshed for publication reliance.
- Bristol-specific Nottingham transferability evidence for displacement, CPZ/RPZ, revenue, congestion, mode shift, employer behaviour and public acceptability.
- DPIA, EqIA, operating procedure, PCN/service controls and appeals workflow.
- OAR, ASR, ASST, model outputs, BCR and value-for-money category.
- OBC assembly, consultation materials, response analysis plan and statutory dossier.

## Do Not Say

- Bristol has approved implementation.
- A boundary, charge, threshold or exemption has been selected.
- Consultation launch is authorised.
- WECA/MCA has approved, consented, objected or has no role.
- Nottingham proves Bristol revenue, congestion, mode shift or employer impact.
- Nottingham, TfL or Leicester evidence means Bristol already has selected, costed, consulted or ready CPZ/RPZ mitigation.
- The statutory submission route is ready.
