# Statutory Route Note

Status: working simulation note. This is not legal advice and is not a statutory confirmation document.

## Working Route

For an initial Bristol Workplace Parking Levy simulation, the apparent statutory spine is Transport Act 2000 Part III Chapter II. A local licensing scheme is made by order of the licensing authority under section 183. For an England-only initial scheme, section 184 appears to require confirmation by the appropriate national authority before the order comes into force, with section 198 pointing to the Secretary of State.

This is a Stage 2 working position only. It must not be used as a final legal conclusion, preferred-scheme authority, or consultation-launch basis until the P0 questions below are resolved by the Legal Review Agent.

## DfT / Secretary Of State Distinction

DfT engagement may help prepare a dossier, but engagement is not statutory confirmation. Any source must be classified as informal officer engagement, policy expectation, procedural requirement, formal decision, or Secretary of State/appropriate national authority confirmation material.
