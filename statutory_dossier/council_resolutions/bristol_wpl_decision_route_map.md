# Bristol WPL Decision Route Map

Status: simulation drafting control. This is not a committee report, officer recommendation, legal advice or public-authority approval.

## Controlled Route

| Product | Decision body in simulation | Officer role | Required wording |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| OBC approval / preferred way forward | Transport and Connectivity Committee | Prepare OBC and advice; no order submission. | Approves OBC/preferred way forward only; does not make a WPL order or launch consultation unless expressly stated. |
| Statutory consultation launch | Transport and Connectivity Committee | Prepare consultation materials, publish and administer under authority. | Authorises formative consultation only; does not predetermine final scheme or make an order. |
| Consultation outcome and preferred scheme | Transport and Connectivity Committee | Analyse responses, update EqIA, appraisal and legal advice. | Notes consultation outcome and authorises next-stage FBC/order drafting only. |
| FBC approval | Transport and Connectivity Committee unless Full Council trigger applies. | Prepare FBC, net-proceeds plans, commercial and operations evidence. | Approves FBC subject to statutory order and confirmation controls. |
| Final WPL licensing scheme order | Route not settled. Transport and Connectivity Committee is the best-evidenced member route for business-case progression but not confirmed for final licensing-scheme order-making. | Prepare order and legal dossier; no officer-only final decision. | Placeholder only until Monitoring Officer/legal route confirmation identifies the decision body. |
| Submission for Secretary of State confirmation | Route not settled. Future decision must authorise submission and named signatory. | Monitoring Officer or authorised officer signs/authenticates/submits only under confirmed authority. | Placeholder only until submitter and signatory route is confirmed. |

## Mandatory Advice Check

Each report must record:

- Monitoring Officer advice on powers, decision-maker, budget/policy framework and constitution route;
- Chief Finance Officer advice on affordability, financial prudence, net proceeds and unlawful expenditure risk;
- equality, consultation, data-protection and accessibility review status;
- whether Full Council is required and why;
- whether WECA/MCA has any role, assurance dependency or funding dependency;
- whether the matter is on the Forward Plan as a key decision.

## No-Overclaim Control

Do not combine OBC, consultation launch, final order-making and statutory submission into one implied approval. Each approval must be separately requested, evidenced and recorded. Do not use this route map as proof that the final WPL licensing-scheme order-maker or submitter has been confirmed.
