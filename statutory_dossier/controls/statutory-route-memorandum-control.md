# Statutory Route Memorandum Control

Status: Stage 10A control note.
Date: 2026-06-26.

This control note defines the future statutory route memorandum. It is not the memorandum itself and it does not settle the submission route.

## Required Classifications

A future memorandum must distinguish:

- initial order;
- ordinary variation;
- revocation;
- RPI-only variation;
- licence-level administration;
- publication, consultation, inquiry and objection route;
- Bristol decision-maker, signatory and submitter;
- DfT engagement versus Secretary of State confirmation;
- WECA/MCA role, consent/no-role and funding dependency.

## Current Position

Stage 10 statutory submission remains blocked. The initial-order confirmation route is controlled as a working assumption, but the final Bristol order-maker, submitter, signatory, WECA/MCA role and WPL-specific DfT process are not settled.

## Required Future Evidence

- Current-law legal source note.
- Bristol Monitoring Officer route confirmation.
- DfT WPL-specific process source or logged response.
- Decision-box route for order-making and submission.
- Clause-by-clause powers matrix.
- Scheme order, boundary schedule and consultation statement crosswalk.
