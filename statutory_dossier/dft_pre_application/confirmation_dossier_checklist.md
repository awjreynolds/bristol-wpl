## Current Gate Use

This checklist is suitable for gap control only. It is not evidence of statutory submission readiness and is not a DfT-accepted submission checklist.

Stage 2J confirms that generic DfT business-case guidance and TAG may be used for OBC/FBC and appraisal discipline, while `SRC-DFT-0003` and `SRC-DFT-0004` are bounded search-control evidence only. They do not prove the current WPL-specific DfT confirmation route, contact route, dossier format, timetable, inquiry, modification or post-confirmation expectations.
