## Stage 2K Order-Change Consultation Control

Consultation planning must distinguish consultation for making, varying and revoking a WPL licensing scheme. Section 185 powers are mapped as permissive statutory powers, but public-law consultation remains fact-sensitive and must be assessed before any consultation or no-consultation claim.
