# Stage 2E WECA/MCA Role Classification Review

Status: simulated WECA governance, legal and funding-assurance review. This is not WECA/MCA approval, legal advice, funding approval or professional assurance.

## Review Decision

Simulation sign-off with conditions for Stage 2E classification controls.

The Stage 2E pack is acceptable for simulation drafting because it:

- separates strategic alignment from formal approval, support, objection, consent and funding;
- treats MCA assurance as conditional on a funding or assurance trigger;
- identifies the current OBC development budget as Bristol CAZ Fund on current repo evidence, not a formal WECA/MCA WPL funding approval;
- treats Bristol Transport Board/WPL Steering Group material as programme governance only;
- keeps current-law WECA/MCA role unresolved after 2026 amendments;
- blocks no-role language;
- requires Stage 2G bounded public-search controls and funding-dependency classification before Stage 2 can pass.

## Conditions

1. Any OBC/FBC regional-governance text must use Stage 2E controlled wording.
2. No business-case section may state that WECA/MCA consent is required or not required until current-law mapping closes.
3. No business-case section may state that WECA/MCA supports, objects to, funds or approves the scheme without a formal decision source.
4. Each WPL-funded package must have a funding-source and assurance-trigger row.
5. Stage 2 remains no-go until Bristol final order/submission route and WECA/MCA current-law role are closed.

## Simulation Sign-Off

The Stage 2E classification is signed off as a simulation control for evidence language only. It does not sign off WECA/MCA statutory role, formal support, objection, funding, assurance approval, no-role conclusion, OBC readiness, FBC readiness or statutory submission readiness.
