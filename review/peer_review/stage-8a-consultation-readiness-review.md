# Stage 8A Consultation Readiness Review

Status: simulated peer review.  
Date: 2026-06-26.  
Reviewers: Legal Review Agent; Consultation Review Agent; Data Protection Review Agent; Accessibility Review Agent; Consultation Analysis Agent; Red Team.

## Scope

The review tested whether Stage 8A safely creates consultation controls without implying launch, material, public-law, privacy, equality, analysis or statutory readiness.

## Findings

1. The control package is acceptable for simulation purposes only.
2. Consultation launch remains blocked by unresolved legal, governance, WECA/MCA, DfT, spatial, appraisal, OBC, DPIA, equality and operating-procedure controls.
3. No consultation document, questionnaire, public HTML page, consultation report, response dataset or officer-distribution material should be authored in Stage 8A.
4. Future analysis must separate response volume, stakeholder category, evidence quality and strength of argument. Headline percentages are unsafe without coding and representativeness controls.
5. Raw responses, direct identifiers and personal data must stay outside normal repo paths.
6. Alternative-format and limited-digital-access routes remain dependencies, not deliverables.
