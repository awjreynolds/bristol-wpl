# Stage 2H Package Funding And Assurance Review

Status: simulated finance, legal and governance review. This is not legal advice, funding approval, WECA/MCA approval, assurance approval, Bristol officer advice, Section 151 certification or professional finance advice.

## Review Decision

Simulation sign-off with conditions for Stage 2H package-level funding and assurance trigger controls.

The Stage 2H pack is acceptable for simulation drafting because it:

- treats WECA/MCA CRSTS, TCR, mass-transit, investment-fund and delivery-assurance material as conditional package-level interface evidence only;
- separates WPL statutory approval from business-case assurance and funding-route approval;
- requires every development cost, implementation cost and WPL-funded package component to carry a funding-source and assurance-route classification;
- prohibits turning strategic context, public-search absence, delivery assurance or programme funding controls into WECA/MCA WPL approval, support, consent, no-role or statutory authority claims;
- keeps `RISK-0002`, `ISS-0002`, `LEGAL-006` and `LEGAL-016` open.

## Findings

| Finding | Severity | Disposition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Current Stage One/OBC development cost is evidenced as Bristol Clean Air Zone Fund, not repo-evidenced MCA WPL funding. | P1 controlled | Use as current development-funding control only. |
| WECA/MCA assurance frameworks apply to their funding streams, programme entry, OBC/FBC approval and transport-scheme OAR/ASR expectations where triggered. | P1/P0 conditional | Treat as conditional assurance-route evidence, not WPL consent. |
| CRSTS early-works, risk-budget movement and delivery-assurance controls can become material if a named WPL package uses or interfaces with CRSTS. | P1/P0 conditional | Add package-level trigger row before OBC/FBC reliance. |
| TCR/LTDP and mass-transit material provide regional programme context and possible package-integration evidence. | P1 controlled | Do not describe as WPL approval or sponsorship. |
| Stage 2G public-search absence does not prove no-role or no-consent. | P0/P1 | Preserve no-overclaim language. |
| Any named MCA funding, match-funding, delegated approval or funding agreement assumed in a cost line becomes decision-critical. | P0 if unevidenced | Block package readiness until formal route and decision source are identified. |

## Conditions

1. Create a package and cost-line funding/assurance classification table before OBC/FBC package reliance.
2. Use the Stage 2H trigger taxonomy for every WECA/MCA reference in Strategic, Financial and Management Case drafting.
3. Do not state that WECA/MCA funds, approves, supports, sponsors, objects to, consents to or has no role in Bristol WPL without a formal source.
4. Do not state that MCA assurance framework relevance is statutory WPL approval.
5. Keep CAZ repayment, net-proceeds accounting, Bristol final order-maker and WECA/MCA statutory role as separate unresolved controls.

## Simulation Sign-Off

The Stage 2H classification is signed off as a simulation control for funding and assurance trigger language only. It does not sign off WECA/MCA statutory role, consent status, formal support, objection, funding approval, assurance approval, no-role conclusion, OBC readiness, FBC readiness, statutory submission readiness or professional financial certification.
