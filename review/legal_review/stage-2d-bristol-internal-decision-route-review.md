# Stage 2D Bristol Internal Decision Route Review

Status: simulated legal, governance, Monitoring Officer and finance review. This is not real legal advice, officer approval or professional assurance.

## Review Decision

Simulation sign-off with conditions for the Bristol internal decision-route map.

The Stage 2D note is acceptable as a simulation drafting control because it:

- uses Policy Committee and key-decision rules as the primary decision-route source;
- identifies Transport and Connectivity Committee as the relevant policy committee for transport-policy, major-project and capital-programme decisions;
- limits officers to preparation and administration unless later legal/Monitoring Officer route confirmation expressly authorises signing or submission;
- requires Monitoring Officer and Chief Finance Officer advice;
- keeps Full Council as a reserved-function, budget/policy-framework and law-reserved check;
- blocks any claim that a real-world route has been legally confirmed.

## Conditions

1. Any OBC, consultation, FBC, final order or submission report must include a discrete decision box.
2. The final order and submission report remains blocked until it identifies the statutory power, decision body, signatory, documents and confirmation condition.
3. Monitoring Officer and Chief Finance Officer advice must be recorded before simulated gate sign-off.
4. If any decision is outside the budget/policy framework or law-reserved, Full Council must be routed explicitly.
5. WECA/MCA role must be resolved before Stage 2 gate completion.

## Simulation Sign-Off

The Stage 2D route is signed off as a simulation control for business-case progression routing only. It does not sign off final order-maker, statutory submitter, consultation readiness, OBC/FBC readiness, WECA/MCA role, spatial boundary, finance, operations or statutory submission readiness.
