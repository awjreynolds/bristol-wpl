# Stage 2H Package Funding And Assurance Legal Review

Status: simulated legal due-diligence review. This is not legal advice, counsel opinion, WECA/MCA approval, Bristol officer advice, statutory confirmation, funding approval or professional assurance.

## Review Decision

Simulation sign-off with conditions.

The Stage 2H materials are acceptable for simulation drafting because they classify WECA/MCA package-level funding and assurance records without converting them into statutory WPL approval, consent, support, sponsorship, funding approval or no-role evidence.

## Legal Control Language

Use:

> WECA/MCA CRSTS, TCR, mass-transit, investment-fund and delivery-assurance records are classified as conditional package-level funding/assurance interface evidence. They may be relevant if a Bristol WPL-funded transport package uses, matches, changes, depends on or interfaces with WECA/MCA funding, programme assurance, delegated approvals or regional delivery arrangements. They do not evidence WECA/MCA approval, support, objection, consent, sponsorship, funding approval, statutory confirmation, WPL order-making power, transferred WPL function or no-role conclusion for the Bristol WPL licensing scheme.

Also keep the Stage 2G bounded-search qualifier:

> The bounded official WECA/ModernGov public-record search on 26 June 2026 did not locate a formal WECA/MCA or Joint Committee decision approving, supporting, objecting to, consenting to, sponsoring or funding a Bristol Workplace Parking Levy. This is a bounded public-record result only, not a no-role or no-consent conclusion.

## Findings

| Finding | Severity | Disposition |

## Rework Triggers

Return any affected document for simulation rework if it states or implies that:

- WECA/MCA approved, supports, objects to, funded, sponsored or consented to Bristol WPL;
- WECA/MCA consent is required or not required;
- WECA/MCA has no role;
- Bristol WPL is a WECA/MCA scheme;
- MCA assurance framework material is statutory WPL approval;
- CRSTS, TCR, mass-transit or investment-programme controls are Secretary of State confirmation;
- a named package has MCA funding, match funding, assurance approval or programme-entry approval without a formal source.

## Simulation Sign-Off

This review signs off Stage 2H as a simulation legal control for package-level funding and assurance wording only. It does not sign off WECA/MCA statutory role, consent status, no-role status, formal support, objection, funding approval, assurance approval, OBC readiness, FBC readiness or statutory submission readiness.
