# Stage 2H Package Funding And Assurance Report

Status: simulation stage-gate report. This is not legal advice, funding approval, assurance approval, WECA/MCA approval, Bristol officer approval, statutory confirmation or professional finance advice.

## Scope

Stage 2H classifies package-level WECA/MCA funding and assurance triggers using existing acquired evidence. It does not run a new public-record search, select a WPL-funded package, create a funding agreement, settle CAZ repayment treatment, resolve Bristol final order-making, resolve WECA/MCA consent or no-role status, or approve any business case.

## Gate Findings

- WECA/MCA CRSTS, TCR, mass-transit, investment-fund and delivery-assurance records are conditional package-level funding/assurance interface evidence only.
- These records may become relevant if a Bristol WPL development cost or WPL-funded package uses, matches, changes, depends on or interfaces with WECA/MCA funding, programme assurance, delegated approvals or regional delivery arrangements.
- These records do not evidence WECA/MCA approval, support, objection, consent, sponsorship, funding approval, statutory confirmation, WPL order-making power, transferred WPL function or no-role conclusion for the Bristol WPL licensing scheme.
- Current acquired evidence identifies the Stage One/OBC development budget as Bristol Clean Air Zone Fund, not as formal WECA/MCA WPL funding approval.
- Any named CRSTS, Single Pot, TCR, mass-transit, investment-fund, CAZ repayment or cross-boundary package dependency becomes P0 if triggered and not supported by a formal funding or assurance-route decision.

## Gate Decision

Stage 2H decision: **simulation sign-off with conditions for package funding and assurance trigger classification; simulation no-go remains because WECA/MCA no-role, consent, consultation-response and funding-dependency conclusions are not closed, and Bristol final order/submission route remains unresolved.**

## No-Go Controls

The following statements remain blocked:

- WECA/MCA has no role.
- WECA/MCA consent is required or not required.
- WECA/MCA has approved, supported, objected to, sponsored, consented to, funded or assured the Bristol WPL.
- Bristol WPL is a WECA/MCA scheme.
- CRSTS, TCR, mass-transit, Investment Fund or Delivery Assurance records are Bristol WPL statutory approval.
- Stage 2G public-search absence proves no-role, no-consent or no-funding-dependency status.
- A named WPL-funded package has MCA funding, match funding, assurance approval or programme-entry approval unless a formal source is registered.
