# Stage 2E WECA/MCA Role Classification Report

Status: simulation stage-gate report. This is not legal advice, WECA/MCA approval, Bristol officer approval, statutory confirmation, financial sign-off or professional assurance.

## Scope

Stage 2E tests WECA/MCA role evidence. It does not resolve Bristol final WPL order-making, DfT procedural expectations, boundary, appraisal, consultation, finance, procurement, operations or statutory confirmation readiness.

## Gate Findings

- WECA/MCA strategy documents may be used as strategic context for demand management and public transport funding.
- MCA assurance framework evidence is conditional and only applies if funding or assurance triggers are engaged.
- Current repo evidence identifies the OBC-stage budget as Bristol Clean Air Zone Fund, not as formal WECA/MCA WPL funding approval.
- Bristol Transport Board/WPL Steering Group evidence is programme governance context only, not a substitute for public committee, MCA or Secretary of State approval.
- No repo-local evidence establishes WECA/MCA formal approval, support, objection, consent, sponsorship, funding decision, transferred WPL function or no-role status.
- Current scoped sources do not support a transferred or concurrent WECA/MCA WPL function, but no-role and no-consent conclusions remain blocked pending consolidated current-law mapping.
- No WPL-specific WECA/MCA consultation requirement or formal consultation response is evidenced in scoped sources.
- Current-law role after the 2026 amendments remains unresolved.
- A current meeting-record search and funding/assurance dependency matrix are required before Stage 2 can pass.

## Gate Decision

Stage 2E decision: **simulation sign-off with conditions for WECA/MCA evidence-language control; simulation no-go remains because WECA/MCA current-law role, funding/assurance dependencies and Bristol final order/submission route remain unresolved.**

## Register Effects

- `RISK-0002` and `ISS-0002` remain P0 but move to narrowed-open classification.
- `EG-0003`, `EG-0010` and `EG-0011` remain partially closed or open as role-mapping controls, not settled conclusions.
- `ASM-0012`, `CLM-0013`, `SSO-0028`, `SSO-0029`, `APP-0009` and `DEC-0004` record Stage 2E controls.

## No-Go Controls

The following statements remain blocked:

- WECA/MCA approved, supports, objects to, funded, consented to or sponsored the Bristol WPL.
- WECA/MCA consent is required or not required.
- WECA/MCA has a transferred or concurrent WPL function.
- WECA/MCA has no role.
- MCA assurance framework evidence is statutory WPL approval.
- Strategic alignment is formal approval.
- Bristol programme-board governance is formal Bristol committee approval, WECA/MCA approval or Secretary of State confirmation.
