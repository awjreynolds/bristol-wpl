## Gate Finding

Stage 3A creates the control architecture for future strategic assessment and investment-programme classification. It reduces the risk of predetermined WPL reasoning, unsupported benefits, false WECA/MCA funding assurance, and premature package reliance.

Stage 3A does not create a Strategic Case, completed problem definition, baseline, objective set, theory of change, investment package, benefit claim, funding decision, WECA/MCA position, consultation proposition, OBC/FBC recommendation or statutory submission readiness.

## Decision

Decision: simulation sign-off with conditions for Stage 3A controls only; simulation no-go remains for Stage 3 strategic assessment gate, OBC/FBC strategic case readiness, consultation reliance and statutory submission readiness.

Conditions:

- future Strategic Case drafting must cite completed problem, baseline, objective, stakeholder, theory-of-change, dependency and benefits evidence;
- every package or cost line must record funding source, WECA/MCA trigger class, formal decision source, assurance route and gate effect before reliance;
- any MCA funding, MCA assurance, CRSTS, Single Pot, TCR/LTDP, mass transit, programme-entry, delivery-funding or programme-change trigger without a formal route is P0 for package reliance;
- gross levy receipts must remain classified as transfer/public cash-flow rather than economic benefit;
- future agents must not claim WECA/MCA approval, support, objection, funding, consent, sponsorship, statutory confirmation, no-role or no-consent without formal source evidence.
