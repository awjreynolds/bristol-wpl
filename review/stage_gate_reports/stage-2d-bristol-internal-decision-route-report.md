# Stage 2D Bristol Internal Decision Route Report

Status: simulation stage-gate report. This is not legal advice, officer approval, statutory confirmation, financial sign-off or professional assurance.

## Scope

Stage 2D maps the Bristol internal decision route for simulation drafting. It tests only Bristol constitutional routing. It does not resolve WECA/MCA role, boundary, parking inventory, consultation materials, modelling, finance, procurement, operations or statutory confirmation.

## Gate Findings

- Key decisions are controlled as Policy Committee decisions and must be forward-planned.
- Transport and Connectivity Committee is the source-supported relevant policy committee for transport-policy, major-project, local-transport-plan, capital-programme and departmental-risk decisions.
- Officers can prepare and administer work within delegated functions, but delegated powers do not override statutory requirements or constitutional controls.
- Final order-making and Secretary of State submission are not yet expressly allocated and must remain P0 until legal/Monitoring Officer route confirmation identifies the decision body and submitter.
- Monitoring Officer and Chief Finance Officer advice are mandatory simulation controls.
- Full Council is a check where budget/policy-framework, law-reserved or constitutional interpretation triggers apply.
