# Stage 2I Final Order And Submission Route Report

Status: simulation stage-gate report. This is not legal advice, Monitoring Officer advice, Bristol officer approval, statutory confirmation, financial sign-off or professional assurance.

## Scope

Stage 2I tests whether the local evidence closes the Bristol final WPL licensing-scheme order-maker, statutory submitter and signatory route. It does not search for new external sources, obtain real Monitoring Officer advice, resolve WECA/MCA role, or approve any order or submission.

## Gate Findings

- Local evidence supports a Bristol-led licensing-authority working assumption and a Transport and Connectivity Committee route for OBC/progression/FBC decisions.
- Local evidence does not expressly allocate the final initial WPL licensing scheme order-making decision.
- Local evidence does not expressly allocate the Secretary of State submission/signatory route.
- Monitoring Officer document authentication can support execution after a valid decision; it is not itself a substitute for the final member decision.
- Full Council cannot be ruled out generically and must remain a live check in any final order/submission report.
- Stage 2I creates mandatory final order/submission decision-box wording and controlled route options, but does not close the P0.

## Gate Decision

Stage 2I decision: **simulation sign-off with conditions for final order/submission decision-box controls; simulation no-go remains because Bristol final order-maker, statutory submitter and signatory route is not closed.**

## No-Go Controls

The following statements remain blocked:

- Bristol City Council is confirmed as competent WPL authority.
- Transport and Connectivity Committee is confirmed to make or submit the final WPL order.
- Officers can make or submit the final WPL order without express source and resolution.
- Full Council is definitely not required.
- Secretary of State confirmation is a formality.
- OBC approval, consultation launch, FBC approval, final order-making and statutory submission are one implied approval.
