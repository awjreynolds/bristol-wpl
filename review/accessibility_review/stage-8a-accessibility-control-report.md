Reviewed Stage 8A consultation controls for material-format, alternative-format, limited-digital-access and response-route safeguards.

## Findings

Stage 8A is acceptable as a control package only. It does not create accessible consultation materials and does not approve a questionnaire, privacy notice, public HTML page, Easy Read document, large-print document, translation, audio route or offline response route.

Launch remains blocked because accessibility decisions depend on the EqIA, stakeholder evidence, material content, privacy controls, response routes and tested publication formats.
