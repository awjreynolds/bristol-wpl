# Source Note: SRC-HMT-0001

Status: Simulation-only source note.
Source: HM Treasury Green Book 2026.
Local source: `evidence/raw/hm-treasury/src-hmt-0001_src-hmt-0001-the-green-book-2026.html`

## What This Source Can Support

- General appraisal and evaluation discipline for public spending decisions.
- Strategic, economic, financial and risk-control expectations for future business-case work.
- Evidence that benefits, costs, transfers, optimism bias, uncertainty and distributional issues need structured treatment.

## What This Source Must Not Be Used To Claim

- That a Bristol WPL has a positive BCR or value-for-money category.
- That gross levy receipts are economic benefits.
- That the business case is Green Book compliant before project-specific appraisal is complete.

## Source-Use Notes

Use as the appraisal framework spine. TAG, databook and local model evidence are still required for transport appraisal reliance.

Does not close ISS-0007.
