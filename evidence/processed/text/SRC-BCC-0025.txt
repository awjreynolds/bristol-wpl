(d) Decision taken

That the Committee for Transport and Connectivity:

1. Noted the outcomes of the review of evidence and best practice and the technical
road by-road assessment outlined in Appendix A.

2. Approved (Unanimously) launching a six-week public engagement on the outcomes
of that technical assessment.

Chair of Transport and Connectivity Committee, to consider the outcomes of the public
engagement and, informed by this, make a delegated decision on which of the proposals
should or should not proceed to the statutory Traffic Regulation Order (TRO) process,
which includes further statutory consultation.

(a) Subject: Incentive Funding
(agenda item 9)

(b) Ward: Citywide
(c) Declarations of interest:

None

(d) Decision taken

That the Committee for Transport and Connectivity (Unanimously APPROVED):

1. Noted the proposed indicative funding allocations of £2,467,388 Incentive funds

2. Authorised the Executive Director Growth and Regeneration, in consultation with
the Chair of Transport and Connectivity Committee, to take all steps required to accept
and spend up to of £2,467,388 of Incentive funds.

3. Authorised the Executive Director Growth and Regeneration, in consultation with
the Chair of Transport and Connectivity Committee to take all steps necessary to
procure and award any contracts (including any contracts over the key decision
threshold) necessary to deliver the works associated with this funding.

4. Authorised the Executive Director Growth and Regeneration, in consultation with
the Chair of Transport and Connectivity Committee to make reasonable changes to the
programme of works to ensure delivery of the programme, in accordance with the
Council’s scheme of financial delegations.
